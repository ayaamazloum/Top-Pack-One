Our Story

From Foundation to Future

History and Establishment

In 2014 a decision was made by The Yassin Brothers to add a new industrial member to the Yassin Holding Group and the result was Top Pack One. Thru out time Top Pack One became a key player in cardboard industry and packaging market thanks to the high-quality products and professional services.

Present and Future

Nowadays, Top Pack One is one of the top companies that manufacture cardboard and offer high quality packaging solution by serving a huge bundle of clients and strategic partners in Lebanon, MENA, Guld and South America. At Top Pack One we are in a continuous process of upgrades, research and development toward factory & production, innovation & designing, and all of our services to maintain our peak position. An expansion plan and strategy has been settled down to add a new production line in order to increase our production capacity that will help Top Pack One enter new markets.
